Interviews

  • Interviews take place over 2 days

    • One day is for the MD-PhD interview. The applicant interviews with the Admission Committee and then has multiple 30 minute interviews with research faculty whom the applicant indicated on their secondary application.

    The other day consists of two interviews with the medical school faculty and tours the campus

Other

  • GRE Required?

    • No

  • There is an MD-PhD Admissions and Advisory Committee that evaluates applicants. Admissions are rolling.

    • MD-PhD students meet annually with the director to review progress and receive feedback

  • Admission decisions are made on a rolling basis.
